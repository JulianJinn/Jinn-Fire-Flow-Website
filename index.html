The Skills

What You'll Learn

Everything is broken down step by step — no experience needed. By the end of the day you'll understand the physics of fire breathing, how to handle fuel and torches safely, and be ready to practise on your own.

01

Safety Protocols

Fire safety fundamentals, spotting, extinguishing, and what to do if something goes wrong.

02

Fuel & Equipment

What fuel to use, what to avoid, and how to properly maintain your torches.

03

Breath & Technique

This is where you learn the technique and you practice with water.

04

Fire Breathing

This is where you will breathe fire for the first time and learn how to move forward as a fire breather.
